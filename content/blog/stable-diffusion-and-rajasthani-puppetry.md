Over the last few weeks I have developed an obscene interest in StableDiffusion. The mathematics itself is fascinating but using it is more of an art than craft (at least currently). Using it has a feeling of mysticism to it. Responses to prompts are not stable (pun intended). Combining them feels like two plus two is a bushel of potatoes.

I have lots to share but here is something which completely blew my mind. I have Rajasthani heritage and am sad the art of puppetry seems to be dying.

I had always wondered given enough budget will the puppeteers come up with high quality modern models and what would they look like. I finally have some answers.
